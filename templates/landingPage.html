What is Feed-a-friend?

About

Dedicated to helping students in need

As the semester progresses, students tend to realize that they have more meal swipes then they could use. Since These swipes disapear at the end of the semester, we have a solution to use up your excess meal swipes. Feed-a-Friend is a social media site that helps link students with excess meal swipes with students in need.

It's simple: just set your profile to either giving or taking. From there, you can either set you availability, if giving, or search for people who are available to give you swipes if you're receiving!
